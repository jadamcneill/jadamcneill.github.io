  • Band: Paramore
  • Album: Paramore
  • Year Released: 2013
  • Instruments: Keyboards, Piano, Bass, Guitar, Strings
  • Fun Fact: (One of those) Crazy Girls was the first Paramore song that used strings

    • This is Paramore’s first album since 2009. This record shows extreme growth from Brand New Eyes, from Hayley’s vocal ability, to the new sounds introduced by Paramore. Paramore convey how they can make happy pop songs, but still know how to go back to their rock roots. This album conveys the evolution of Paramore.

    Our Rate: 10/10

  • Band: HAIM
  • Album: Something to Tell You
  • Year Released: 2017
  • Instruments: Guitar, Bass, Keyboards, Drums, Percussion, Bottle, Strings, Saxophone
  • Fun Fact: Want You Back was originally started as a slow song, then they sped it up.

    • As a sophomore record, this is an amazing record. HAIM shows off new genres to the plate with this record. They include R&B elements and a more soft rock tone to this album. They convey the loneliness, sadness, and realizations of being in relationships.
